Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Description

A delicious broccoli cheddar soup that is filled with carrots, broccoli, garlic, and cheese. Rich, thick, and creamy that is perfect during cold weathers It will take less than 30 minutes. You won't regret it.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter, ½ stick
  • ½ medium onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoon AP flour
  • 2 cups low sodium chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp , or ground nutmeg, optional
  • 3 cups broccoli florets, or 1 large head, cut into small pieces
  • 1 large carrot, grated, julienned or finely chopped
  • 2 cups half & half, or milk or light or heavy cream
  • 8 oz block grated cheddar cheese, or 2 cups (mild, medium, or sharp)
