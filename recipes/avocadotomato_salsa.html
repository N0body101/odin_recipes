Avocado & Tomato Salsa

Description

A simple diced avocado, tomato, red onion, and cilantro salsa that is perfect to eat on tortilla chip. It's a quick 15 minutes recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, pitted and diced
  • 1 cup tomato, diced (any type of tomato)
  • ¼ cup onion, diced
  • ¼ cup cilantro, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Add the avocado, tomato, onion, and cilantro to a large mixing bowl. Add olive oil, fresh lime juice, and pinch of salt and pepper. Gently mix until it fully combines.
  2. Serve immediately or keep in the refrigerate for up to 2 days. The lime will keep the avocado from browning.
